## 🎯 Key Setting Features

### Unique Mechanics
- **Veil Resonance:** 5% chance of random magical effects when casting
- **Planar Exposure:** Extended time in Wilds causes transformation
- **Death & Return:** Easier resurrection but with "Echoes" (planar memories)

### Core Themes
- Adaptation vs. Control
- Identity in flux
- The cost of survival
- Hope in darkness

### Major NPCs
- **Mira Vance:** Idealistic researcher (catalyst)
- **Elias Vance:** Legendary Veil-Walker (mentor)
- **Vex:** Secret-keeping navigator
- **Warden Sylva:** Voice of natural wisdom
- **Forge-Thane Obsidia:** Reformist craftsperson
- **Magister Thaddeus:** Ruthless pragmatist
- **Nerissa:** The hidden eighth (main villain)
- **The Seven Echoes:** Transformed archmages

## ✨ Design Philosophy

**Critical Role-level depth means:**
- Rich NPCs with conflicting motivations
- Moral complexity (no pure heroes or villains)
- Lore that emerges through play
- Player choices that reshape the world
- Emotional stakes alongside combat challenges
- A world that feels lived-in and responsive
