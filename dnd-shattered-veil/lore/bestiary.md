# Bestiary of the Shattered Veil

## Overview

The Shattering didn't just change the world—it changed its inhabitants. New creatures have emerged from planar bleeding, existing species have mutated, and things that shouldn't exist now stalk the Fractured Wilds.

---

## Veil-Touched Beasts

Creatures mutated by exposure to raw planar energy.

### Veil Wolf
*Medium beast, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 37 (5d8 + 15)  
**Speed** 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 16 (+3) | 15 (+2) | 16 (+3) | 3 (-4) | 12 (+1) | 6 (-2) |

**Skills** Perception +3, Stealth +4  
**Damage Resistances** One random type (fire, cold, lightning, or psychic)  
**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13  
**Languages** —  
**Challenge** 1 (200 XP)

**Planar Adaptation.** The veil wolf has resistance to one damage type, determined randomly when the wolf is encountered. Roll d4: 1=fire, 2=cold, 3=lightning, 4=psychic.

**Pack Tactics.** The wolf has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

**Actions**  
**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

**Veil Strike (Recharge 5-6).** The wolf channels planar energy into its bite. The next attack deals an extra 7 (2d6) damage of its resistance type.

### Fractured Bear
*Large beast, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 12 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 68 (8d10 + 24)  
**Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 19 (+4) | 10 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 2 (-4) | 13 (+1) | 7 (-2) |

**Skills** Perception +3  
**Damage Immunities** One random type  
**Damage Vulnerabilities** One random type  
**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13  
**Languages** —  
**Challenge** 3 (700 XP)

**Unstable Biology.** The fractured bear's relationship with planar energy is volatile. When the bear is encountered, roll twice on the damage type table (d10: 1-2=acid, 3-4=cold, 5-6=fire, 7-8=lightning, 9=thunder, 10=force). The bear is immune to the first result and vulnerable to the second.

**Multiattack.** The bear makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Actions**  
**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage.

**Claws.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (3d6 + 4) slashing damage.

**Reality Roar (Recharge 6).** The bear releases a blast of unstable planar energy in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 14 (4d6) damage of the bear's immune type on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

## Planar Hybrids

Creatures born of merged planar and material essence.

### Fey-Stag
*Large fey, chaotic neutral*

**Armor Class** 14 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 52 (7d10 + 14)  
**Speed** 60 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 15 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) |

**Skills** Perception +5, Stealth +6  
**Damage Resistances** Psychic  
**Condition Immunities** Charmed, Frightened  
**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 15  
**Languages** Sylvan, Common  
**Challenge** 3 (700 XP)

**Fey Ancestry.** The fey-stag has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put it to sleep.

**Glamour.** The fey-stag can cast *charm person* at will (spell save DC 14).

**Legendary Resistance (1/Day).** If the fey-stag fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Actions**  
**Multiattack.** The fey-stag makes two attacks: one with its antlers and one with its hooves.

**Antlers.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) psychic damage.

**Hooves.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (2d4 + 4) bludgeoning damage.

**Dream Walk (Recharge 5-6).** The fey-stag can teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. When it does so, each creature within 10 feet of its starting or ending location must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or be charmed until the end of their next turn.

## Maw-Touched

Those who've spent too long near The Maw and been transformed.

### Echo-Walker
*Medium aberration, chaotic neutral*

**Armor Class** 12  
**Hit Points** 39 (6d8 + 12)  
**Speed** 30 ft., fly 30 ft. (hover)

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 16 (+3) | 16 (+3) |

**Skills** Arcana +4, History +4, Perception +5  
**Damage Resistances** Psychic, Force  
**Condition Immunities** Charmed, Frightened, Paralyzed  
**Senses** Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 15  
**Languages** Common plus 1d4 random languages  
**Challenge** 3 (700 XP)

**Incorporeal Movement.** The echo-walker can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

**Memory Theft.** When the echo-walker hits a creature with an attack, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or lose one memory (determined by the DM). The echo-walker gains proficiency in one skill the target had until the end of its next turn.

**Actions**  
**Multiattack.** The echo-walker makes two Memory Touch attacks.

**Memory Touch.** *Melee Spell Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (2d6 + 3) psychic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by Memory Theft.

**Unstable Phasing (Recharge 5-6).** The echo-walker becomes fully incorporeal until the start of its next turn. It can move through any obstacle and is immune to all damage except force and psychic.

Creatures from the Elemental Chaos that have adapted to the Material Plane.

**Armor Class** 13  
**Hit Points** 60 (8d10 + 16)  
**Speed** 0 ft., fly 40 ft. (hover)

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |

## Using These Creatures

### Encounters in the Fractured Wilds
- Veil-touched beasts are common—use them as random encounters
- Planar hybrids often have their own agendas beyond simple predation
- Maw-touched should be rare and significant—encounters with them advance the plot

### Threshold City Encounters
- Fractured cultists operate in secret, especially in the Depths and Shanties
- Some planar hybrids have learned to pass as normal in cities (especially fey-stags)
- Echo-walkers are usually drawn to places of strong memory or emotion

### Environmental Storytelling
- Describe how creatures have adapted to planar conditions
- Use mutated beasts to signal nearby planar bleed zones
- Let players learn to "read" the wildlife—veil wolves' resistance indicates nearby energy types
