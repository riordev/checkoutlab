# Magic in the Shattered Veil

## Overview

The Shattering didn't just break the world—it changed how magic works. The Veil, once a barrier that regulated planar energy, is now permeable. Magic is more powerful than ever, but also more dangerous, more personal, and more alive.

---

## The Nature of Magic Now

### Planar Bleeding
Magic now draws directly from the planes that bleed through the torn Veil. When a wizard casts *fireball*, they might be channeling elemental fire, infernal flame, or radiant energy from the Upper Planes—the spell looks similar, but its source matters.

**Mechanics:** When a spell is cast, the DM may (or the player may request) roll d20. On a 1, the spell triggers a **Veil Resonance** (see below).

### Veil Resonance
Unpredictable magical feedback caused by the damaged barrier between planes.

**Veil Resonance Table (d100):**

| Roll | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 01-05 | **Planar Echo:** The caster's shadow acts independently for 1 minute |
| 06-10 | **Essence Leak:** The caster glows with colored light (matching their spell school) for 1 hour |
| 11-15 | **Whispers:** The caster hears voices from another plane for 10 minutes (disadvantage on Wis checks) |
| 16-20 | **Gravity Shift:** Gravity changes direction for the caster for 1 round |
| 21-25 | **Temporal Hiccup:** The caster acts last in initiative next round |
| 26-30 | **Elemental Surge:** All creatures within 10 feet take 1d6 damage (type matches spell school) |
| 31-35 | **Memory Fragment:** The caster remembers something from another life (DM provides vision) |
| 36-40 | **Planar Attraction:** A minor elemental appears and follows the caster for 1 hour |
| 41-45 | **Spell Echo:** The spell affects the target twice (second casting at half damage/effect) |
| 46-50 | **Veil Tear:** A tiny rift opens for 1 round (sucking in unattended objects within 5 feet) |
| 51-55 | **Arcane Feedback:** Caster takes 1d12 force damage |
| 56-60 | **Emotional Amplification:** The caster's emotions are heightened (roleplay effect for 10 minutes) |
| 61-65 | **Language Shift:** The caster can only speak an exotic language for 1 minute |
| 66-70 | **Transparency:** The caster becomes partially translucent for 1 minute (advantage on stealth) |
| 71-75 | **Magnetic:** Metal objects are attracted to the caster for 1 minute |
| 76-80 | **Plant Growth:** Plants in 30 feet grow rapidly (difficult terrain) |
| 81-85 | **Time Dilation:** Time moves differently for the caster (1 round = 6 seconds for others, 1 minute for caster) |
| 86-90 | **Summon Whimsy:** A harmless but distracting fey creature appears |
| 91-95 | **Reality Anchor:** The caster is immune to planar effects for 1 minute |
| 96-100 | **The Veil Opens:** The caster glimpses another plane (DM determines effects) |

### Stability Fields & Magic
Within Threshold Cities' Stability Fields, magic is more controlled:
- Veil Resonance only occurs on a natural 1 (instead of standard 5%)
- Some spells may be restricted or require licenses
- Planar-touched magic is viewed with suspicion

In the Fractured Wilds:
- Veil Resonance occurs on 1-5
- Some spells are enhanced (DM's discretion based on nearby planar bleed)
- Wild magic zones may apply additional effects

## New Spells

### 1st Level

**Veil Strike**
*1st-level evocation*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous  

You channel raw planar energy into a beam of crackling force. Make a ranged spell attack against a target. On a hit, the target takes 2d8 force damage and must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

---

### 2nd Level

**Stabilize Field**
*2nd-level abjuration*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self (30-foot radius)  
**Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of salt)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour  

You create a zone of stabilized reality around yourself. Within this area:
- Veil Resonance cannot occur
- Planar effects are suppressed
- Creatures have advantage on saving throws against planar phenomena

This spell is considered illegal in some Threshold Cities without proper licensing.

---

### 3rd Level

**Planar Binding, Lesser**
*3rd-level abjuration*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V, S, M (a silver chain worth 100 gp)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You attempt to bind a creature from another plane to your will. Target a celestial, elemental, fey, or fiend within range. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be charmed by you for the duration. While charmed, the creature must obey your commands to the best of its ability.

The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

---

### 5th Level

**Echo of Self**
*5th-level illusion*  
**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** V, S, M (a mirror)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You create a planar echo of yourself—a translucent duplicate that shares your space. While the spell lasts:
- You can use a bonus action to have the echo move up to your speed and make one weapon attack
- The echo uses your statistics and can be targeted by attacks (AC = your AC, 1 HP)
- If the echo is destroyed, you take 2d10 psychic damage
- You can switch places with the echo as a bonus action

## Magic Items

### Veil-Touched Items

Items created or altered by planar energy, often with unpredictable properties.

**Shardblade**
*Weapon (any sword), rare (requires attunement)*

This blade appears to be made of crystallized planar energy. It deals an extra 1d6 force damage on a hit. Once per day, when you hit with this weapon, you can choose to deal maximum damage instead of rolling.

**Curse:** Each time you use the maximum damage feature, roll on the Veil Resonance table.

---

**Cloak of Shifting**
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*

This cloak changes color to match your surroundings, granting advantage on Stealth checks. Once per day, you can use a bonus action to become invisible until the start of your next turn or until you attack or cast a spell.

**Veil-Touched:** When you use the invisibility feature, there's a 20% chance you instead teleport up to 30 feet in a random direction.

---

**Essence Storage Crystal**
*Wondrous item, rare*

This crystal can store spell energy. Any spellcaster can expend a spell slot to store that slot level in the crystal (maximum 5 levels). A spellcaster holding the crystal can expend stored levels to cast spells without using their own spell slots.

**Complication:** Each time a level is expended, roll d20. On a 1, the crystal releases stored energy in a 20-foot radius explosion (1d6 force damage per level stored).

---

**The Cartographer's Compass**
*Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement)*

This compass doesn't point north—it points toward the nearest planar bleed, rift, or anomaly. It can also detect the direction of Threshold Cities within 50 miles.

Once per day, you can use an action to learn the type of planar energy at a detected location (Elemental, Fey, Shadow, etc.).

---

**Ring of Echoes**
*Ring, rare (requires attunement)*

This ring allows you to communicate with Echoes—ghostly remnants of the past. Once per day, you can cast *speak with dead* without using a spell slot or material components.

Additionally, you can see and hear Echoes naturally, even when they're not trying to manifest.

**Curse:** You sometimes see Echoes that aren't there (DM's discretion), which can be distracting or alarming.

---

### Forge-Bound Items

Items created by the artificers of the Crucible and similar settlements.

**Adaptive Armor**
*Armor (any), rare (requires attunement)*

This armor automatically adjusts to protect against threats. When you take damage, the armor gains resistance to that damage type until you take damage of a different type. You can only have one resistance active at a time.

---

**Construct Companion**
*Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement)*

A small mechanical creature (statistics as a homunculus) that serves as a companion. It can:
- Understand and obey complex commands
- Record and playback up to 1 hour of audio
- Store up to 3 levels of spells (castable by the attuned user)
- Self-repair 1 HP per hour

If destroyed, the companion can be rebuilt with 8 hours of work and 500 gp of materials.

---

**The Crucible Key**
*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement)*

A masterwork tool created in the Heartforge itself. It can function as any artisan's tool, thieves' tools, or navigator's tools. Additionally:
- You have advantage on checks made with this item
- Once per day, you can cast *knock* or *mending* (your choice)
- It can open any non-magical lock in 1 round

## Magical Phenomena

### Reality Storms
Severe planar weather that affects entire regions. Types include:

**Memory Rain:** Precipitation composed of liquid memories. Those caught in it experience random visions from other people's lives.

**Gravity Shear:** Areas where gravity changes direction unpredictably. Navigation requires Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks to avoid falling damage.

**Probability Fog:** Mist where chance is heightened. All d20 rolls are made with advantage and disadvantage simultaneously (roll twice, use the third die).

**Elemental Surge:** Storms mixing fire, water, earth, and air in impossible ways. Deals 2d6 damage per round of exposure (type changes each round).

### Wild Magic Zones
Areas where the Veil is particularly thin. Spells cast here trigger Wild Magic Surges on a roll of 1-3 on d20. Additionally, uncontrolled magical effects occur randomly:

| d10 | Effect |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | All creatures in zone switch bodies for 1 minute |
| 2 | Gravity reverses for 1 round |
| 3 | Everyone speaks only Sylvan for 10 minutes |
| 4 | Plants animate and attack (stats as awakened shrubs) |
| 5 | Time loops—everyone repeats their last action |
| 6 | Everyone's appearance changes to match the nearest creature |
| 7 | Spells cast heal instead of harm (and vice versa) |
| 8 | Metal objects become magnetic, flying toward each other |
| 9 | Everyone gains telepathy with each other for 1 hour |
| 10 | A random planar entity manifests, confused and angry |

## Spell Modifications

### Summoning Spells
All spells that summon creatures (conjure animals, summon elemental, etc.) have a 25% chance of summoning something slightly wrong:
- Elementals may have merged with other elements
- Fey may be from the Bleeding Court (with their own agendas)
- Undead may retain memories of their past lives
- Celestials and fiends may be desperate refugees rather than loyal servants

### Divination Spells
Information gained through divination is increasingly unreliable:
- **Augury** results are accurate 75% of the time (instead of reliable)
- **Divination** answers may be cryptic or refer to possible futures rather than certainty
- **Commune** contacts may be planar entities pretending to be gods
- **Scrying** has a 20% chance of showing the target's location in an alternate timeline

### Teleportation Spells
Travel between locations is more dangerous:
- **Misty step** and **dimension door** work normally
- **Teleport** has a 25% chance of arriving slightly off-target (1d10 × 100 feet in random direction)
- **Teleportation circle** may connect to the wrong circle if the destination has been moved or damaged
- **Plane shift** always deposits travelers 1d10 miles from their intended destination on the target plane
