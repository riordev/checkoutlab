# The Fractured Wilds

## Overview

Between the Threshold Cities lies the **Fractured Wilds**—a landscape where the Shattering's effects are most pronounced. Here, reality itself is in flux. Travel is dangerous but necessary, and those who master the Wilds command immense value.

---

## Traveling the Wilds

### Navigation
Traditional maps are useless in the Fractured Wilds. Landmarks shift, distances change, and paths that existed yesterday may be gone today. Successful navigation requires:

**Veil-Walker Techniques:**
- Reading planar energy flows (like reading wind or currents)
- Following ley lines—invisible channels of magical energy
- Using Wayfinders to detect stable paths
- Trusting intuition over instruments

**DCs for Navigation:**
- Following a known Road: DC 10
- Finding a hidden path: DC 15
- Crossing uncharted territory: DC 20
- Navigating during a reality storm: DC 25

### Travel Pace
| Terrain | Effective Speed |
|---------|----------------|
| Stable path | Normal |
| Unstable ground | Half speed |
| Planar bleed zone | Quarter speed (or variable) |
| Reality storm | Cannot travel (must shelter) |

## Environmental Hazards

### Reality Storms
Weather events where planar energy manifests physically.

**Memory Rain:** Liquid memories fall from the sky. Being caught causes visions (Wis save DC 12 or be incapacitated for 1 minute).

**Gravity Shear:** Gravity changes direction unpredictably. Every hour, roll d8 to determine "down."

**Probability Fog:** All d20 rolls are made twice; results interact unpredictably.

**Elemental Surge:** Storm mixing all elements. Deals 2d6 damage per minute of exposure.

### Planar Bleed Zones
Areas where another plane's rules have replaced the Material Plane's.

| Zone Type | Effects |
|-----------|---------|
| **Feywild Bloom** | Time moves differently, emotions heightened |
| **Shadowfell Gloom** | Light dimmed, despair aura, undead common |
| **Elemental Chaos** | Random elemental damage, terrain shifts |
| **Astral Echo** | Thoughts manifest visually, psionic phenomena |
| **Infernal Contract** | Deals are binding, truth compelled |
| **Abyssal Corruption** | Madness risks, physical mutations |
