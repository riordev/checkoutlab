# The Shattered Veil: Quick Reference

## Campaign Overview
- **Setting Name:** The Shattered Veil
- **Premise:** 300 years after the Veil between planes shattered, magic leaks unpredictably, and civilization survives in protected "Threshold Cities"
- **Tone:** Dark fantasy with hope
- **System:** D&D 5e with modifications

## Key Mechanics

### Veil Resonance
When casting a spell, roll d20. On a 1, roll d100 on the Veil Resonance table (see Magic section) for a random magical effect.

- In Threshold Cities: Only on natural 1
- In Fractured Wilds: On 1-5
- In The Maw: On 1-10

### Planar Exposure
Extended time in the Fractured Wilds causes changes:
- **Stage 1:** Cosmetic (glowing eyes, shadow movement)
- **Stage 2:** Mechanical (resistances, new senses)
- **Stage 3:** Transformation (partially becoming something else)

### Death & Resurrection
- Resurrection is easier (borders to afterlife are thin)
- The returned may bring back **Echoes** (memories from other planes, other lives)

## Player Options Summary

### New Races
- **Veil-Touched:** Planar-exposed humanoids with unstable gifts
- **Echo Born:** Returned from death with planar fragments
- **Fey-Blooded:** Feywild-human hybrids from post-Shattering bleeding
- **Rootbound:** Symbiotic plant-humanoids from Thornhaven-like environments

### New Subclasses
- **Barbarian:** Path of the Fractured Rage
- **Cleric:** Domain of the Veil
- **Druid:** Circle of the Thornbloom
- **Sorcerer:** Planar Soul
- **Warlock:** The Shattered Patron

### New Backgrounds
- **Veil Refugee:** Survivor of a collapsed settlement
- **Concordat Agent:** Former (or current) city operative
- **Wilds Guide:** Navigator of the Fractured Wilds

## Campaign Arc: "Echoes of Tomorrow"

### Act I: The Fractured World (Sessions 1-4)
- Discovery of stable rifts
- Introduction to factions
- Revelation of the Final Fracture

### Act II: The Hidden Truth (Sessions 5-8)
- Uncovering Nerissa's existence
- Traveling to The Maw
- Learning the true nature of the Shattering

### Act III: The Final Fracture (Sessions 9-12+)
- Gathering allies and resources
- Confronting the Seven Echoes
- Deciding the fate of reality

## Key NPCs

### Allies
- **Mira Vance:** Researcher, catalyst for adventure
- **Elias Vance:** Legendary Veil-Walker, mentor
- **Vex:** Navigator Guildmaster, secret Conspiracy member
- **Warden Sylva:** Thornhaven leader, voice of nature
- **Forge-Thane Obsidia:** Reformist, pragmatic ally

### Antagonists
- **Magister Thaddeus:** Ruthless but conflicted
- **The Seven Echoes:** Transformed archmages, each with agenda
- **Nerissa:** The hidden eighth, architect of Final Fracture

## Environmental Hazards

### Reality Storms
- Memory Rain: Visions of other lives
- Gravity Shear: Changing gravity
- Probability Fog: Doubled dice rolls
- Elemental Surge: Mixed elemental damage

### Planar Bleed Zones
- Feywild Bloom: Time dilation, emotional amplification
- Shadowfell Gloom: Despair, undead
- Elemental Chaos: Random damage, terrain shifts
- Astral Echo: Manifested thoughts, psionics

## The Central Question

*In a broken world, what will you preserve? What will you become?*

Every session should engage with this theme in some way.
