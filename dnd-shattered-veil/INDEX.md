## 🎯 Quick Start for DMs

### New to the Setting?
1. Read [README.md](./README.md) for the overview
2. Read [world-overview.md](./world-overview.md) for the history
3. Skim the 5 locations to understand the world
4. Review [QUICK-REFERENCE.md](./QUICK-REFERENCE.md) for session use

### Running Your First Session
1. Start with characters in **Sunderpoint** (neutral ground)
2. Use Adventure Hook #1 (The Refugee Caravan) as introduction
3. Introduce Veil Resonance early to establish tone
4. End with a choice that reveals faction tensions

### Key Mechanics to Track
- **Veil Resonance:** Roll when spells are cast
- **Planar Exposure:** Track time in Fractured Wilds
- **Faction Reputation:** Note how NPCs react to player choices

## 🔧 Customization Notes

This setting is designed to be adapted:
- Add or remove factions based on your campaign focus
- Adjust the timeline (300 years post-Shattering is default, not mandatory)
- Scale the Final Fracture threat to match your desired campaign length
- Create new planar bleed zones with unique effects
- Expand any location with additional districts and secrets
